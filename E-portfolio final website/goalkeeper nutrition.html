Being a goalkeeper your diet can make a difference in the math result and whether your team is going to win or lose. As a goalkeeper there are a few musts that you have to follow when it comes to nutrition. Eating more vegetables will help you maintain peak performance and your mind won't start to lose focus and concentration. As a goalkeeper you also need to eat higher quality carbohydrates so that you have the energy you need for the match day.
