Midfielder Workouts/ Excercises

Excercises
The midfield position requires a balance of skills and strengths. Since they play in the middle of the pitch they require a bit of everything. They must have speed, agility, strength and power.

Running and Turning
Run diagonally then make a turn to the left side and then the right side.

Lunge maps
Lunge forward with your right leg then lunge sideways with your right leg. Then lunge back with your left leg and sideways with your left leg.

Lateral jumps
Jump as far as you can sideways and keep your other leg in the air while doing the same.

Split Squat
Use a bench to keep your foot back. Then lower your body. Your knee should be bent when you lower your body.

Box step ups
Place one leg on top of a box. Then drive through with the opposite leg upwards.
