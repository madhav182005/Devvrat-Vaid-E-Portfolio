Crunch, Twists, Hurdles and Sprint
Lay down on the floor and perform a full crunch. As soon as you complete that do 4 russian twists. Do some hurdles and then sprint.

Biceps curl
Pick up a weight in your hand (whatever weight you are comfortable with) and curl your hand inwards.

Box jumps
Stand on the floor. Jump on a box.
